What is it?
Messenger assistant
Bot is a messenger companion created to automatically assist customers and provide them support and another relevant information. They work either in fully automatic mode or in semiautomatic where bots answer partially based on human decisions.
New way to communicate with clients
Millions of people use messengers every day. All you need is an automatic assistant which talks to your clients there, solves routine issues and provides clients requested information. They will get just what they want. Bot talks to customers using one of the popular messengers, such as Telegram, Facebook Messenger or Skype.
